Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing VanturazTravel Pvt. Ltd. for your online airways ticket booking needs. We strive to provide you with a convenient and hassle-free experience. However, we understand that plans may change, and you may need to cancel or modify your flight reservation. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding cancellations, refunds, and changes to your bookings. Please read this policy carefully before making any reservations.

1. Cancellation and Refund Eligibility:

1.1. Non-Refundable Bookings:

a) Some bookings may be non-refundable or have specific cancellation policies imposed by airlines or third-party vendors. These policies may apply to certain fare types, promotional offers, or special deals. We will clearly indicate the non-refundable nature of such bookings during the reservation process.

1.2. Refundable Bookings:

a) Refundable bookings are subject to the terms and conditions of the airline or third-party vendor's refund policy. These policies may vary depending on the fare type, route, and time of cancellation.

b) We will assist you in processing refundable bookings in accordance with the applicable refund policies.

2. Cancellation and Refund Process:

2.1. Cancellation Requests:

a) If you wish to cancel your booking, you must notify us by contacting our customer support team through our designated channels. Please provide the necessary details, such as your booking reference number and personal information, to facilitate the cancellation process.

2.2. Refund Requests:

a) After canceling your booking, you may request a refund by contacting our customer support team. We will review your request and initiate the refund process in accordance with the applicable refund policy.

2.3. Refund Processing Time:

a) The processing time for refunds may vary depending on the airline or third-party vendor's policies. We will make every effort to expedite the refund process, but please note that it may take several business days for the refund to be processed and reflected in your account.

3. Changes to Bookings:

3.1. Change Requests:

a) If you need to make changes to your booking, such as date or time modifications, please contact our customer support team. We will assist you in making the necessary changes subject to the availability and terms set by the airline or third-party vendor.

3.2. Change Fees:

a) Changes to bookings may be subject to change fees imposed by the airline or third-party vendor. These fees are determined by their respective policies and may vary depending on the fare type, route, and timing of the change request. We will inform you of any applicable change fees before processing the changes.

4. Exceptions and Limitations:

4.1. Force Majeure:

a) In case of unforeseen circumstances beyond our control, such as natural disasters, political unrest, or airline strikes, refunds and changes may be subject to additional terms and conditions set by the airline or third-party vendor. We will assist you to the best of our abilities under such circumstances.

4.2. Other Limitations:

a) This refund policy does not cover any additional charges or fees imposed by the airline or third-party vendors, such as baggage fees, seat selection fees, or service charges. Please refer to the respective terms and conditions provided by the airline or third-party vendor for information regarding these additional charges.

5. Contact Us:

For any inquiries or assistance regarding cancellations, refunds, or changes to your booking, please contact our customer support team through the following channels:

  • Email: contact@vanturaztravels.com
